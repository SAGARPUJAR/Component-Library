Avatar

Avatar Demo

Use the Avatar Component as a graphical representation of a person through a profile picture or image, an icon, or a string with initials

Type

  1. Large - Suitable for profile pages
  2. Medium - Appropriate for custom menus and visualizations
  3. Small - Easily embedded in a contact list and similar repetitive scenarios

Shape

The avatar comes in two distinct types determined by its shape: a round and a square form.
